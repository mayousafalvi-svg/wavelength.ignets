Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about how Ignets sources, evaluates and delivers 10G, 100G and 400G Layer 1 wavelength and dedicated internet access services.

Ignets aggregates demand from many enterprises, service providers and platforms that all require high-capacity Layer 1 and DIA connectivity. This allows us to negotiate from a wholesale position rather than as a single buyer. We maintain ongoing benchmark data across hundreds of routes, terms and capacities, so we can quickly identify when a quote is misaligned with current market levels.

In practice, customers typically see savings of around 35% on new deployments and renewals. For some routes and terms, particularly where there are multiple viable carriers, we see savings upwards of 40–45%. Because we are vendor-neutral, our incentives are aligned with finding the most competitive mix of carriers, not steering you to a specific provider.

Our core focus is on high-capacity Layer 1 wavelength services and dedicated internet access (DIA). We routinely source 10G, 100G and 400G circuits for data center interconnect, cloud on-ramp, backbone and specialized low-latency use cases. Depending on the carrier and region, we can also help with supporting Ethernet and IP services that complement those wavelengths.

Geographically, we work across major metros, long-haul corridors and subsea systems in North America, Europe and beyond. If you are unsure whether your particular route or capacity is in scope, reach out and we will quickly confirm coverage and indicative pricing ranges based on our live data.

Claude helps us ingest, normalize and analyze large volumes of quote data from different carriers. Every provider formats pricing, terms and technical details differently, which often makes manual comparison slow and error-prone. With Claude, we standardize these inputs and highlight the most relevant differences for your requirements.

This means you receive a coherent comparison table that captures not just price but also term flexibility, SLAs, installation intervals, diversity characteristics, cross-connect implications and more. Human network and sourcing experts remain in the loop at every step, using Claude as an accelerator and quality-assurance layer rather than a black-box decision maker.

Most customers choose to work with Ignets under a single master services agreement, which significantly simplifies commercial, legal and operational management. Under this model, we handle the relationships with individual carriers on your behalf while you benefit from a consolidated interface for billing, support and lifecycle management.

In some scenarios—such as very large, strategic routes or specific compliance requirements—we can support hybrid models where you contract directly with certain carriers while still using our market intelligence and sourcing capabilities. We will discuss the best structure for your organization early in the engagement.

To get started, we typically need your endpoints (data center names and addresses or on-net building info), required capacities (10G, 100G, 400G), target timelines, term preferences and any specific technical constraints such as maximum latency, diversity requirements or preferred carriers. If you already have quotes or existing contracts, sharing those helps us benchmark and validate potential savings more quickly.

From there, we translate your inputs into a structured brief for our supplier ecosystem, coordinate responses, and deliver a clear recommendation pack. You can reach us at Quotes@ignets.com to initiate a project or ask clarifying questions before engaging formally.
